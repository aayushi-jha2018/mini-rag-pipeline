New Employee Onboarding Guide — Data & Engineering Team

Before your first day
IT will email you a laptop setup link and temporary VPN credentials three business days before your start date. Complete the security training module linked in that email before day one.

Day one checklist
Report to reception by 9:30 AM for badge photo and building access setup. Your manager will walk you through team structure, current projects, and your first two-week ramp-up plan.

Access requests
Request access to Git repositories, the data warehouse, and internal dashboards through the Access Portal. Most standard access is approved automatically within one hour; elevated access requires manager sign-off and can take up to two business days.

Development environment setup
Clone the team starter-kit repository and run the setup script, which installs the required Python version, package manager, and pre-commit hooks. Reach out in the #eng-onboarding channel if the setup script fails.

Code review norms
Every change needs at least one approval before merging. Reviews are expected within one business day. Use draft pull requests for early feedback on work in progress.

On-call rotation
New engineers join the on-call rotation after 90 days, always shadowing an experienced engineer for their first two rotations before going solo.

Who to ask
Your manager is your first point of contact for role and project questions. For access or tooling issues, post in #eng-onboarding. For anything else, the #general channel is a good place to ask.
