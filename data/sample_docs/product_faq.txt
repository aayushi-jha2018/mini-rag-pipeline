Cloud Storage Plan — Frequently Asked Questions

What plans are available?
We offer three plans: Starter (50 GB), Team (500 GB), and Business (2 TB). All plans include automatic daily backups and end-to-end encryption for files at rest.

How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Go to Account Settings > Billing > Change Plan. Upgrades take effect immediately and are billed pro-rata for the current cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my storage limit?
You will receive an email warning at 90% usage. Once you exceed 100% of your limit, uploads are paused until you free up space or upgrade your plan. Existing files remain accessible.

Can I share files with people outside my organization?
Yes. Any file or folder can be shared via a link with view-only or edit permissions. Admins can restrict external sharing at the workspace level from Account Settings > Security.

How long are deleted files kept before permanent removal?
Deleted files move to Trash and are kept for 30 days before permanent deletion. You can restore any file from Trash during that window.

Is there a limit on individual file size?
The Starter plan supports files up to 5 GB. Team and Business plans support files up to 50 GB per upload.

What regions is data stored in?
By default, data is stored in the region closest to your account signup location. Business plan customers can pin storage to a specific region from Account Settings > Data Residency.
